Juyue Productivity

Juyue provide a wide range of services, from concept development and feasibility studies to 3D modeling and prototyping.

Some common projects include designing new products, redesigning existing products, and developing concepts for new product lines. In each case, the goal is to create products that are safe, functional, and appealing to consumers. The industrial product designer must have a strong understanding of engineering, ergonomics, and aesthetics.
