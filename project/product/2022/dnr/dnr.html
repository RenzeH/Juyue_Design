The epidemic has gradually eased, and everyone has gone out. To prevent cross-infection, most people Avoid using public transportation to work and travel. If you want a car that can do both For daily commuting, you can also ride it for outdoor adventures? DNR has scoda mode as well as cross Cycling mode, which can quickly switch functions, allowing users to meet the needs of commuting And play the spirit of outdoor adventure!

↓ Render capability.

The straddle bike mode is for solo riding. There is a function like a fuel tank cover that can be clamped during extreme driving. Realize the integration of people and vehicles, and enjoy the fun of cycling alone. After switching to scooter mode, it makes getting into the car easier. A back seat has also been added to shuttle between cities with partners.
